There are two ways to view Email Headers in Outlook 2010

  1. Opening the Email

    • Open the email you want to view the headers for. Then click the File menu.

    • Click the Properties button in the menu

    • A new window opens, displaying the headers

  2. Adding a Button to QAT

    • Click the down arrow at the top of the ribbon and select More Commands

    • Change the drop down box to All Commands

      >

    • Search for message options then click add

    • Finally select the email you wish to view and click the new icon
